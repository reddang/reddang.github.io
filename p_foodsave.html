The Story

SINGAPORE have wasted about 788,600 tonnes of food yearly reported. Out of this only 13% is recycled. Singapore spends a lot of its money in buying food and costs around $14.8 billion in buying food from outside countries, being one of the top 10 countries in the world with highest rate of food wastage.

Solution

An App platform with sharing economy system which enable B2C & C2C connected to each by integrates food sellers and purchasers with about-to-expiry notifications on good deals.

Features

Merchants can list their “before expiry” products on the APP real-time and alert nearby consumers to purchase in discounted prices.
                            
As household is one of the main segment which contribute of total food wastage. Users can list their “extra” cooked food or even newly cooked food before expiry on the App for nearby users to purchase and consume.
