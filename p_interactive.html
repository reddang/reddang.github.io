What's Interactive E-Commerce?

Catergory: Business Insight Project: UNA Market Client: OnPoint Date: December, 2021

Project Description

Project name: UNA Market – Group Shopping Platform
Role: Product Owner / Product Manager
Overview: UNA Market is an innovative e-commerce platform designed to transform the way people shop for food and daily essentials. By integrating group purchasing mechanics, UNA Market empowers customers to enjoy high-quality VietGAP-certified products at significantly reduced prices, while fostering a fun and social shopping experience with friends and family.

Key Highlights:
- Daily Shock Deals: Discounts up to 50% on popular items such as Ri6 durian, pork belly, and Korean strawberries.
- Group Buying Advantage: Prices at least 20% lower than market rates when purchased in groups.
- Free Shipping: Available for all orders within Ho Chi Minh City.
- Quality Assurance: Products meet VietGAP standards, ensuring freshness and safety.
- Fast Delivery: Orders delivered within 24 hours.

Mission: To eliminate consumer concerns about price and quality in online food shopping, while creating a joyful, community-driven shopping experience.
Tagline: “The more people, the cheaper it gets – shopping made social and fun.”
